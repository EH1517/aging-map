## Downloaded & Processed Sources

### 1. NCES Projections of Education Statistics to 2031
- **Table**: Digest Table 203.20
- **Coverage**: All 50 states + DC, state level
- **Years**: Fall 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025, 2031 (actual); 2026-2030 interpolated; 2032-2050 extrapolated
- **Method**: NCES National Elementary and Secondary Enrollment Projection Model

### 2. California Department of Finance, 2025 Series
- **Coverage**: 58 California counties
- **Years**: 2024-25 through 2046-47 (22 projection years)
- **Method**: Grade progression ratio (cohort-survival) model
- **File**: `data/ca_k12_enrollment_2025.xlsx`

### 3. Iowa Department of Education, Certified Enrollment Projections
- **Coverage**: 99 Iowa counties (aggregated from ~330 districts)
- **Years**: 2025-26 through 2029-30 (5 projection years)
- **Method**: Grade progression rate + birth data
- **File**: `data/ia_enrollment_projections.xlsx`

### 4. Maryland Department of Planning, School Enrollment Projections 2025
- **Coverage**: 24 Maryland jurisdictions (23 counties + Baltimore City)
- **Years**: 2024 actual + 2025-2034 projected (10 projection years)
- **Method**: Population projections + grade succession methodology
- **File**: `data/md_enrollment_projections.xlsx`

### 5. Pennsylvania Department of Education, District Enrollment Projections
- **Coverage**: 67 Pennsylvania counties (aggregated from 499 districts)
- **Years**: 2025-26 through 2034-35 (10 projection years)
- **Method**: Grade progression + modified enrollment rate model
- **File**: `data/pa_enrollment_projections.xlsx`
